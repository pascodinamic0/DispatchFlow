# DispatchFlow

DispatchFlow is a modern internal logistics and procurement management platform built for organizations operating across multiple cities, branches, and provinces.

The platform enables teams outside the capital city to request operational items from headquarters, track procurement progress, monitor shipment movement between locations, and confirm deliveries through a centralized real-time system.

Designed specifically for operational realities in emerging markets and distributed organizations, DispatchFlow replaces fragmented WhatsApp coordination, manual followups, and poor shipment visibility with a structured operational workflow.

## Core Features

* Multi-branch request management
* Procurement tracking
* Inter-city shipment tracking
* Real-time operational timeline
* Role-based access control
* Delivery confirmation workflows
* Activity and audit logs
* File and invoice uploads
* Mobile-first responsive dashboard
* Analytics and operational insights
* Realtime notifications

## Use Cases

DispatchFlow is ideal for:

* NGOs
* Mining companies
* Telecom companies
* Schools and universities
* Hospitals and clinics
* Construction firms
* Distribution companies
* Organizations operating across multiple provinces or regions

## Vision

DispatchFlow is more than a dispatch tracker.

It is an operational infrastructure platform designed to help organizations improve accountability, procurement visibility, logistics coordination, and multi-branch operational efficiency across Africa and emerging markets.
