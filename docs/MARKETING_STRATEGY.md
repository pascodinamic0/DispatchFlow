# DispatchFlow Marketing Strategy

**Product:** Internal logistics & procurement platform (procurement requests, dispatch, inventory).  
**Tagline:** Request. Track. Deliver.  
**Live site:** https://dispatch-flow-one.vercel.app/

## Ideal customer profile (primary)

**Who:** Operations, procurement, and dispatch leaders at **multi-branch enterprises** in African and emerging markets (10–500+ employees in ops scope).

**Job to be done:** Replace spreadsheets + WhatsApp + legacy ERP gaps with one **system of record** the field actually uses.

**Segments (priority order):**

1. Operations / supply chain leads — need KPIs and SLA visibility  
2. Procurement teams — need approvals, priority, audit trails  
3. Dispatchers / fleet coordinators — need assignments and mobile status updates  

**Industries:** FMCG distribution, pharma/medical supply, construction materials, NGO logistics, retail chains, manufacturing internal transfers.

**Geographies:** DRC, East Africa, West Africa, Southern Africa, plus global HQs managing regional ops.

**Not for:** Consumer marketplaces, single-location shops, teams happy with one shared sheet.

## Positioning (StoryBrand / growth-operator)

| Element | Message |
|--------|---------|
| **Hero (customer)** | Ops leader drowning in manual status reports |
| **Problem** | External: multi-branch chaos. Internal: “I never know what’s in transit.” Philosophical: Teams deserve software as clear as Stripe, not ERP theater. |
| **Guide** | DispatchFlow — calm UI, RLS security, mobile-ready |
| **Plan** | Request → Track → Deliver (3 steps on site) |
| **CTA** | Primary: Create free workspace. Transitional: Blog, Contact |
| **Failure** | Missed deliveries, stockouts, audit gaps, leadership flying blind |
| **Success** | One dashboard every morning; field updates in real time |

## Channel plan (implemented on-site)

- **Landing:** Contrast section, audience ICP, proof, FAQ, CTA funnel  
- **Blog:** SEO + trust for ops keywords (`/blog`)  
- **SEO:** `sitemap.xml`, `robots.txt`, Open Graph, canonical URLs  
- **Next (optional):** LinkedIn posts from blog excerpts, Google Search Console, case study PDF lead magnet
